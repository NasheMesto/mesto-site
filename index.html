About MESTO

MESTO is a cultural space, community restaurant, and artist-run guesthouse on Koh Phangan, Thailand. We host workshops, queer balls, cinema nights, open mics, and more — over 500 free events since 2022. Our space includes a coworking zone, DIY tools, 3D printer, projectors, sewing machines, art supplies, and resident animals.

MESTO is a prototype for the future: a maker space, creative home, and a decentralized network of cultural hubs. Built by artists, creators, and friends — for the curious, the bold, and the kind.

What We Do

  • 🎬 Cinema Cult – outdoor screenings of cult films
  • 🎨 Sketch it Easy – live figure drawing with music
  • 🎵 Music Jam – open mic and spontaneous concerts
  • 🧶 Workshops – tufting, silkscreen, soft soldering
  • 🍜 Restaurant – cozy dinners & garden evenings
  • 🌈 Queer Balls & Parties – inclusive & unforgettable

Our Vision

We’re building new MESTO spaces in cities like Berlin, New York and São Paulo. Alongside our island hub, we’re developing:

  • 🎨 On-chain art marketplace
  • 🏡 Community-funded artist residency
  • 🧠 DAO governance and tools
  • 🌐 A metaverse twin for global connection
